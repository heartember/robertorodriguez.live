Working Together

A liberatory offering for teams, leaders, and movements—meeting conflict, change, and complexity with depth and skill.

Who I support

  • BIPOC/PGM communities engaged in systemic change for their lives and futures
  • Teams working for justice and collective liberation
  • Leaders tending burnout, groups navigating rupture, communities building cohesion

Challenges we meet

  • Trauma-informed, power-conscious leadership development
  • Dialogue, repair, and trust-building after tension
  • Working with rank, positionality, and institutional dynamics
  • Somatic and ritual practices that strengthen connection

Best outcomes come over time

While I can support moments of acute conflict or change, my work is most effective through longer-term, developmental partnerships that build trust and sustainable transformation.

Relational Wholeness

An integrated framework spanning personal healing and collective transformation.

Four interconnected levels

  1. Personal: self-awareness, growth, and care
  2. Interpersonal: communication, conflict transformation, relationship-building
  3. Systemic: shifting institutional and social dynamics
  4. Transpersonal: meaning, ritual, ancestral memory, the living world
