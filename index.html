Schedule A Call Relational Wholeness My Approach to Coaching and Consulting Relational Wholeness is the path I walk and the framework I share. It describes the key areas of human experience where I offer support. My Approach I help groups and organizations work at four interconnected levels for lasting change: ‚Ä¢ Personal: self-awareness, growth, and care. ‚Ä¢ Interpersonal: communication, conflict transformation, relationship-building. ‚Ä¢ Systemic: understanding and shifting social and institutional dynamics. ‚Ä¢ Transpersonal: connecting to shared meaning, ritual, ancestral memory, and the living world. This integrated approach moves beyond quick fixes, building real depth, resilience, and capacity for change. The Four Dimensions of Relational Wholeness If I were to describe these levels as separate categories (which they are not), I would say this: The Dreaming Body (Inner Work & Healing) This is the realm of inner life‚Äîbody, psyche, and personal history. Here we explore beliefs, patterns, nervous-system states, and personal traumas, while nurturing your deepest longings and gifts. My coaching supports you to work compassionately with your internal systems so you can align your actions and practices with your most life-affirming values and goals. The Space Between Us (Relational Practice) This is the field of relationship‚Äîwhere we meet friends, family, colleagues, and communities. Here, we practice the art of connection: finding common ground, collaborating, celebrating, and navigating conflict. My coaching and consulting help individuals, couples, groups, and organizations identify the influences shaping their dynamics and build more generative, collaborative ways of working together. The Liberation Promise (Systems & Social Change) This path explores freedom from oppressive patterns while embracing healthier, life-giving alternatives in culture, institutions, and social systems. My work supports individuals and organizations to recognize the often-invisible forces that shape our perspectives and behaviors, and to co-create new, liberatory ways of being, relating, and organizing. The Dreaming World (Non-Ordinary States & Wisdom) This is the realm of imagination, altered states, and non-ordinary perceptions‚Äîa domain well-known to traditional healers and mystics. For individuals, my coaching invites you to pay attention to your own non-linear, non-rational experiences as sources of insight, inspiration, and connection to the wider living world. For groups and organizations, I help facilitate shared meaning-making practices, collective rituals, and imaginative approaches that deepen cohesion, cultural grounding, and a sense of purpose beyond the everyday.
